Jialei Wang

My name is Jialei Wang, I am a PhD candidate from Cornell University and majored in Operations Research. My research interest is Bayesian Global Optimization, more specifically, the exploration vs. exploitation tradeoff for sequential decision-making under uncertainty.

I like coding, and I see it as a way to transform ideas into reality. I did an internship as a software engineer at Yelp in the summer of 2014, and during the internship I contributed to the open-sourced software package for Bayesian global optimization MOE.

You can find me on Twitter, GitHub, and Facebook. Here is my Curriculum vitae.
